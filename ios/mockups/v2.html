Key Design Changes from v1

  • Pure OLED black background — saves 40% battery
  • iOS systemGray scale for cards — feels native
  • Electric Mint accent — pops harder than forest green
  • iOS system colors for semantics (blue, red, green, etc.)
  • 20px border radii — matches iOS 18 design language
  • 0.33px separators — Apple's exact separator weight
  • Blur-backed tab bar — native vibrancy feel
  • SF Pro weight scale — 300 to 900
3 New Features
Mobile-first capabilities that maximize efficiency, speed, and usability
01
Snap & Ship
Efficiency
Open camera → snap → AI generates full listing in 2 seconds → one-tap publish to all platforms. The entire listing-to-live flow in under 10 seconds. No typing required.
  • Real-time camera viewfinder with AI object detection overlay
  • Auto-generates title, description, category, brand, size, condition, price
  • Shows confidence score and 3-tier pricing (Quick Sale / Fair / Premium)
  • One-tap "Publish All" sends to every connected platform simultaneously
  • Progress pipeline shows each step: Snap → Analyze → Optimize → Publish
  • Offline queue — snap without internet, auto-publishes when back online

02
Offer Blitz
Speed
Tinder-style offer management. Incoming offers stack as cards. See AI profit analysis, market comps, and buyer history at a glance. Accept, Counter, or Decline in one tap.
  • Card stack UI — swipe right to accept, left to decline
  • AI recommendation with confidence score on every offer
  • Real-time profit calculator (revenue - cost - fees - shipping)
  • Market comp data: "This item sells for $70-90 on avg"
  • Counter with AI-suggested amount pre-filled
  • Batch mode: review 20 offers in under a minute
